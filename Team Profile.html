Personality Comparison

Personality can generally reflect how well an individual can work in a group. For our group ‘Simple People’, we can make comparisons with the results of 3 different personality tests to get a basic understanding of the compatibility between our group members. The diverse personality types of every group member allows each of us to continue or complement each other’s works. For example, we all have a learning style of being a visual learner, this suggests that for all of us to gain an understanding of new notions, we should work with our strengths by understanding concepts visually. Our mixed personality types for the Myer-Briggs test illustrate diversity in our group and with diversity we can work to each other’s strengths and combat each others weaknesses. The groups’ unified personalities can have the added bonus benefit of having the best of all worlds with no drawbacks.
